Cahoots Configuration Change this file to suit your installation’s needs.

debug = False¶
To disable a module, simply comment it out of this list.
Be aware that modules may have soft-dependencies on one another, and a disabled module may impact results results from other modules which seek its “council.” This may result in unexpected confidence scores, a change in result determination, etc.
